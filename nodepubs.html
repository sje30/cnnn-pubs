UK Node publications

This page lists some recent publications from members of the UK node. It is not meant to be exhaustive but shows the breadth of neuroinformatics work performed by some of our members.

If you are part of the UK neuroinformatics community and wish to add a publication to the list, just email Stephen Eglen the DOI and/or pubmed reference. Please do not send more than one or two papers so that we maintain a diversity of publications from different research groups.
